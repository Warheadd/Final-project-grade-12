What method of transportation was used?

Gas car

A privately owned vehicle that is primarily for the road. This includes vehicles such as cars, trucks and vans. The vehicle can not be powered by an electric motor, it must use a fuel that has/contains a carbon based fuel which include gasoline and petrol .

Electric car

A privately owned vehicle that is powered by an electric motor and primarily for the road.

Bus

A large public motor vehicle that can carry multiple passengers. It generally follows a fixed route and requires a fare.

Walking/biking

Travelling on foot or using a vehicle that has no motor and is human powered on the road or sidewalk. This includes the average bicycle, scooter, skateboard or etc.

Airplane

A vehicle that travels through the air through the use of wings, propellers, engines and/or thrusters.

Train

A large vehicle that usually consists of multiple cars and follows a specific rail system.

Boat

A vehicle that travels across water using an engine or sail.
